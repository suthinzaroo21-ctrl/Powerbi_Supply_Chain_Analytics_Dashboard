# Powerbi_Supply_Chain_Analytics_Dashboard
Tools: Power BI, Excel

This Power BI supply chain analytics report template helps supply chain managers, logistics operations teams, and executives in the Food & Beverage industry monitor overview performance, supplier analysis and warehouse & inventory analysis.
Designed for unique F&B industry, this report delivers visibility across three core areas:
•	Overview Performance: Tracks high-level KPIs, fulfillment rates, OTIF compliance and YoY cost trends.
•	Supplier Analysis: Evaluates vendor lead times, OTIF compliance, and sourcing risks.
•	Warehouse & Inventory: Monitors turnover, stockout risks, and spoilage to minimize waste.

## Dashboard Analysis
## KPIs included
•	Supply Chain Performance Overview of Revenue, Gross Profit, OTIF %, Late Deliveries, Stockouts with KPI cards
•	Customer segmentation of Profit Performance 
•	Revenue and Profit analysis with Risk Tier Comparison
•	Suppliers, warehouses, or channels are underperforming 
•	Yearly, Monthly, Quarterly trends of OTIF %, Late Deliveries, Stockouts

## Key Insights
•Monitor Earliest Date, Revenue and OTIF % by Year, Quarter, Month and Day
•Analyze Customer Segment Performance Summary
•Explore Revenue Breakdown by Category, Subcategory, and Product Name
•Examine Revenue and Profit by Country, Supplier Name and Risk Tier
•Show Expired Quantity Breakdown by Category, Product, and Shelf Life Group

## Who this report is for:
Supply chain managers, logistics operations teams, and executives
